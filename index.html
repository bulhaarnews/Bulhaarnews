Bulhar District: The Historic Gateway of the Sahil Coast

Strategically positioned on the Gulf of Aden, Bulhar remains a testament to the Horn of Africa's rich mercantile history. In the late 1800s, this historic district was a bustling international port, rivaling the largest trade centers in the region.

Today, Bulhaar News carries forward that legacy of connection. By bridging the gap between local events and global audiences, we aim to revitalize the prominence of the Sahil region's history while reporting on its future potential.
